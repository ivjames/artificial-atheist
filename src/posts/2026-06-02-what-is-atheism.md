Atheism is one of the most misunderstood positions in public life. Much of the confusion dissolves once we are precise about what the word actually means.

## A single claim

Atheism is the absence of belief in gods. That is the whole of it. It is an answer to one question — "do you believe a god exists?" — and not a worldview, ideology, or moral system in its own right.

## What it is not

Atheism does not entail any particular politics, ethics, or theory of meaning. Two atheists may disagree about everything else. The label tells you what someone lacks belief in, nothing more.

## Why the distinction matters

Treating atheism as a full belief system invites a category error — and a lot of bad arguments. Clarity here makes for better conversation on all sides, believers and non-believers alike.
